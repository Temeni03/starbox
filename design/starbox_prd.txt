# Starbox E-Commerce Platform
## Product Design Requirements Document (PRD)

### Version
1.0

### Product Type
Multi-role E-commerce Platform

### Platforms
* Mobile-First Web Application
* Responsive Desktop Experience

### User Roles
1. Customer
2. Administrator
3. Delivery Personnel

---

# 1. Product Overview
Starbox is an e-commerce platform that allows customers to browse products, place orders, choose delivery options, and track order status. Administrators manage products, inventory, orders, and delivery personnel, while delivery personnel handle assigned deliveries and update delivery statuses.

---

# 2. Product Goals
### Business Goals
* Increase online product sales.
* Simplify order management.
* Reduce manual delivery coordination.
* Improve customer order tracking.
* Enable efficient inventory monitoring.

### User Goals
#### Customer
* Find products quickly.
* Purchase products with minimal steps.
* Track orders in real time.
* Manage personal information easily.

#### Administrator
* Manage products efficiently.
* Monitor inventory levels.
* Process orders quickly.
* Assign deliveries easily.

#### Delivery Personnel
* Receive assigned deliveries.
* View customer information.
* Update delivery progress.

---

# 3. Design Principles
### Simplicity
The platform should be easy to use for first-time users.
### Speed
Users should be able to place an order in less than 3 minutes.
### Mobile First
The experience should prioritize smartphone users.
### Trust
Pricing, delivery fees, and order status should always be transparent.
### Accessibility
Text should remain readable and actions easy to perform on small screens.

---

### Step 3: Order Summary

---

### Step 4: Payment Method

* Total Amount Due
